I'm currently pursuing my Bachelor of Technology in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Sri Sairam Institute of Technology in Chennai, which I started in August 2024. I'm up to my 4th semester with a CGPA of 8.7 out of 10.

Before that, my schooling took me through a few different places:

- **11th–12th (Higher Secondary):** John Dewey Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Villupuram
- **8th–10th (Secondary):** Krishnaswamy Memorial Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Cuddalore
- **Up to 7th (Primary):** E.S. Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Villupuram

## Higher Secondary Education (Class XII)

I completed my Higher Secondary Education (Class XII) from **John Dewey Matriculation Higher Secondary School**, Villupuram, under the **State Board of Tamil Nadu**, appearing for the public examination in **March 2024**.

### Subject-wise Marks

| Subject | Marks |
|----------|------:|
| Tamil | 87 |
| English | 74 |
| Physics | 76 |
| Chemistry | 82 |
| Mathematics | 89 |
| Computer Science | 98 |
**Total Marks:** **506**

One of my strongest academic performances was in **Computer Science**, where I secured **98 marks**, reflecting my growing interest in programming and technology during higher secondary education.
