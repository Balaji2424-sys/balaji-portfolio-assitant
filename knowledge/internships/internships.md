I've completed two internships so far:

## Web Development Intern — Acmegrade Private Limited, Bangalore (in collaboration with Wipro)
**July 10 – September 15, 2025**

This was a 60-day intensive internship focused on real-world application development. I strengthened my full-stack workflows through guided mentorship, and it really enhanced my technical, analytical, and project coordination skills in an industry setting.

## R&D Intern — Sri Sairam Techno Incubator Foundation, Chennai
**April 6 – May 15, 2026**

A 30-working-day offline R&D internship where I contributed to experimental software solutions focused on sustainability, geospatial intelligence, and AI-driven analytical systems. Specifically, I built a GIS-based dashboard and analytical engine to identify water-sustainable landscapes suitable for manufacturing, industrial, and large-scale commercial establishments.

I also explored and developed solutions using MCP servers and LLM-based workflows — including a mini-project that automated Google Drive folder creation and GitHub repository generation through a chatbot powered by Groq LLM integration. That project actually became the seed for my "LLM MCP Automation Assistant" project.
