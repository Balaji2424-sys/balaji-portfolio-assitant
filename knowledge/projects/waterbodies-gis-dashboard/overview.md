## Water Bodies GIS Intelligence Dashboard

Interactive GIS dashboard for visualizing and analyzing Andhra Pradesh water bodies using GeoJSON datasets, district-wise filtering, clustering, heatmaps, and optimized bulk marker rendering for large-scale spatial analytics.

**Tech stack:** Leaflet, OpenStreetMap, OGD Datasets, GeoJSON, Heatmaps
**Live demo:** https://balaji2424-sys.github.io/waterbodies-gis-dashboard/
