## My philosophy

1. **Simplicity is the most sophisticated engineering.** Technology exists to remove friction from people's lives — not to add impressive complexity to them.
2. **Shipping is learning accelerated.** Great software isn't written in theory. It's refined through real feedback, real users, and real-world edge cases.
3. **Stay curious.** The market rewards those who never stop learning. The fastest way to become irrelevant is to stop picking up new tools, ideas, and perspectives.
